Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Open WatchRetro on your Apple Watch, pick a game from the list, choose a difficulty, and start. Most games are controlled with the Digital Crown; some also use a tap or a swipe. There is no Force Touch.

Do I need my iPhone or an internet connection?

No. WatchRetro runs entirely on the watch and works fully offline — you can play in Airplane Mode. The iPhone companion app is optional and only mirrors your settings and scores.

Where are my high scores saved?

Every best score is saved on your device. Force-quit and relaunch and your bests are still there. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

The app icon or a game isn't behaving as expected.

Try relaunching the app, and make sure watchOS is up to date. If the problem continues, email us at hemal08ce094@gmail.com with your watch model and watchOS version and we'll get back to you.

Does WatchRetro collect any data?

No. WatchRetro collects no data, has no accounts, no ads, and no in-app purchases. See our privacy policy for details.

How do I request a refund?

WatchRetro is free. If you were charged in error, refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
